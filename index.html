we are all cyborgs

The upcoming of new technologies has allowed the wide spreading of new subjectivities, promoting re-meaning on the perception of the social universe and the reordering in every spheres of post-modern life with deep cultural and social-political. Thus, considering the perspectives of Bruno Latour, Donna Haraway, and Chris Gray, the main goal was to discuss the question of hybridism resulting from the interaction man-machine and the relations of such matter concerning the exercise of a new citizenship in the context of the Techno-scientific capitalism.
